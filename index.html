Onigiri Ingredients

Onigiri is typically made with white rice and can be filled with a variety of ingredients such as:

  • Umeboshi (pickled plum)
  • Salmon
  • Tuna with mayonnaise
  • Konbu (kelp)
  • Tarako (cod roe)
