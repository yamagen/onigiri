Simple Overview
  1. Cook Japanese rice:
    Use short-grain rice. The rice should be warm, not cold.
  2. Line a bowl with plastic wrap and put rice on it:
    This time, we use plastic wrap in a bowl and shape the onigiri through the wrap, not with bare hands.
  3. Add the filling:
    Put a filling (for example, salmon or pickled plum) in the center.
  4. Gently shape the rice:
    Do not press hard. Lightly shape it into a triangle or round shape.
  5. Wrap with nori (seaweed), if you like.
    You can wrap it before eating or just before serving.
